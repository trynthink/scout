Market Calculator

Determine the energy use associated with building components, appliances, and other end uses in residential and commercial buildings.

This is currently an incomplete product. The content shown here is intended to demonstrate appearance and provides no functionality. Any apparent outputs or results should be considered inaccurate.

The Market Calculator yields the estimated energy use from losses through the building envelope and appliances and devices within homes and businesses in the United States. This energy use can be divided by building type, climate zone, technology type, and other factors indicated below.

To obtain an estimate for a market or markets of interest, the appropriate definitions must be selected below. In each category shown, at least one selection must be made to yield a complete market definition. In some categories, multiple selections are permitted. Categories where multiple selectons are allowed are indicated as such. Selections for the relevant groups are made by simply clicking the appropriate terms. Selected terms are highlighted, and clicking them again will remove them from the chosen market segment. Follow the numbered steps below, making the desired selections at each step. Once selections have been made in each category, click the 'Update' button in the Market Size box on the right side of the screen to get the size of the selected market.

The underlying data for this calculator are from the 2014 Annual Energy Outlook (AEO) released by the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) .
